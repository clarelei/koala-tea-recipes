ABOUT
the most comprehensive index of recipes for using every last bit of food

FoodMatters is a web-hosted database that displays recipes and uses for leftover food scraps and expired foods, and allows users to search and edit existing information, while adding new entries of their own. Developed at PixelHacks II 2018 Hackathon. Built with Node.js, Heroku, MongoDB, and GupShup. Thanks for visiting! More coming soon.
